About Me Jay Yu

Description

My name is Jay Yu and I am a third year Computer Science major at the University of San Diego. This website is a project for my Culture Art and Technology 125 class. I will use this site as a medium to discuss the broad topic of internet privacy. My intention for creating this website is to consolidate the wide range of information to one source in a light hearted manner.Internet privacy is a looming topic and more individuals should be aware of their internet rights; especially as we transition into a more internet dependent society. All information will be presented in an unbiased fashion to help further develop the readers own personal opinions on this matter of internet privacy.
